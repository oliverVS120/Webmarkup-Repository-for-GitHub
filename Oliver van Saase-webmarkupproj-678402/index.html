Houston, Texas

Houston, Texas is the largest city within Texas, being home to 2.31 million people. With its population being so vast there are many amenities that are available to the people of the city. For instance in the areas of the metropolis there is a Zoo, a fine arts museum, a very famous space center that launched the apollo 11 mission and a Museum of natural history. Furthermore, it is also my hometown, it's where I grew up and spent most of my childhood years there.

Austin, Texas

Austin, Texas is the capital of Texas, and is actually one of the lesser populated cities than the others. For instance, the population in Austin is oonly 950 thousand, while not a small number, if you compare it to Texas in general which has a total population of 29 million, and the other cities which average around 1 million or more, it would be below average.

San Antonio, Texas

San Antonio is another major city within Texas, this city however is more farther inland than both Austin and Houston, and is right in the middle when it comes to population size. However, San Antonio has much more culutural significance than the other two cities. For instance, the slogan 'Remember the Alamo' is often said because during the Texas revolution in 1836 the Alamo was under siege for 13 days and won independence because they overcame the siege.
