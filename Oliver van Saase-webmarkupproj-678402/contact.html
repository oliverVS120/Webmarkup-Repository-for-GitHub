U.A.E., Dubai

Link to booking

Offered by Tui.nl

If you are willing to fork out the cash for this trip you better be prepared to enjoy it, coming in at 1,148 € this includes booking the hotel room and other basic emenities and thats about it, as Dubai is a very expensive city this price point just for a hotel is quite understandable. Grand Hyatt Dubai however is one of if not the most visually beautiful hotels the area has to offer, with it being a five star hotel it comes included with indoor & outdoor swimming pools and allows for guest to have the option of choosing a jacuzzi within their room if they please. Furthermore there is also a dolphinarium a thirteen minute walk away from the hotel that is available to see for guests.
